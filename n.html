Prime Minister Modi Meets President Trump

Prime Minister Narendra Modi and US President Donald Trump met in Washington to strengthen diplomatic ties. Their discussions covered a 10-year defense cooperation plan, boosting trade relations, and increasing technology collaborations. The US aims to sell advanced fighter jets to India, while India seeks US investment in manufacturing and infrastructure projects.
